# Page 1 - Panel 1: 大雄的早晨困扰

## Panel Description
Large establishing panel showing Nobita's bedroom in the morning. Nobita is sitting up in bed with dizziness spiral effects around his head.

## Characters
- **大雄 (Nobita)**: Sitting up in bed, wearing yellow pajamas, confused expression with spinning spiral eyes, dizziness effects around head
- **Position**: Center frame, sitting on bed

## Visual Elements
- **Background**: Japanese-style bedroom with tatami mats, window showing morning sunlight streaming in
- **Dizziness effects**: Concentric spiral lines around Nobita's head (manga style)
- **Bed**: Traditional Japanese futon, messy blankets
- **Window**: Morning sunlight rays, blue sky visible
- **Room details**: Desk with books, clock showing early morning

## Mood & Atmosphere
- Morning light but with confusion/dizziness
- Slightly disoriented feeling
- Warm color palette for sunlight, cool tones for dizziness

## Expressions & Effects
- Nobita: Eyes with spiral patterns (表示眩晕), slight sweat drop
- Dizziness spiral: Dynamic rotating lines around head
- Question marks floating in air

## Text/Dialogue
- Small text box (corner): "早起的困扰..." (Early morning trouble...)
- Sound effect: " Gurururu" (dizziness sound)

## Manga Effects
- Speed lines radiating from center
- Spiral dizziness effect
- Sparkle effects for morning light
- Exaggerated manga expression

## Style Notes
- Manga/anime style (哆啦A梦 style)
- Clean line art

- Expressive character emotions
