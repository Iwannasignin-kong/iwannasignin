# Page 1 - Panel 2: 镜子前的困惑

## Panel Description
Nobita looking at himself in the mirror, pale complexion, thought bubble showing question mark about "unstable blood pressure".

## Characters
- **大雄 (Nobita)**: Standing in front of mirror, pale face, concerned expression
- **Position**: Looking into mirror, reflection visible

## Visual Elements
- **Mirror**: Full-length or wall-mounted mirror
- **Nobita's reflection**: Pale skin, tired eyes, concerned expression
- **Thought bubble**: Large thought bubble with question marks and "血压不稳" (unstable blood pressure) text
- **Background**: Bathroom or bedroom corner, morning light
- **Color palette**: Cool tones for pale/unhealthy look

## Mood & Atmosphere
- Worried, uncertain
- Slightly muted colors to show discomfort
- Quiet contemplation

## Expressions & Effects
- Nobita: Worried eyes, slight frown, pale complexion
- Thought bubble: Multiple question marks, medical symbol + question mark
- Sweat drop on temple

- Thought bubble text: "血压不稳?" (Unstable blood pressure?)

- Thought bubble with question marks
