- **Focus**: Soup pot and time passage
- **Pot**: Center of panel

- **Steam/aroma**:

- **Clock/time passage**:

  - Arrow or transition to 2 hours

- **Pot contents**: Visible through glass lid (if used), rich broth
- **Kitchen**: Warm, cozy atmosphere
