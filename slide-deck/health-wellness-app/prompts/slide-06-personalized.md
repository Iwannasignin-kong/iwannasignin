# Slide 6: Personalized Recommendations - 个性化推荐

## Style Reference
Watercolor style - soft, hand-painted aesthetic with warm off-white background (#FAF8F0), visible brush strokes, and organic shapes.

## Content
Title: 个性化推荐，因时制宜
Features:
- 按体质推荐
- 二十四节气养生
- 立春吃春饼
- 清明食青团
- 每个时节的养生智慧

## Visual Composition
- Top: Hand-painted title
- Center: Circular seasonal wheel with 24 solar terms
  - Each section painted with different watercolor washes
  - Spring highlighted (立春, 清明 visible)
- Surrounding illustrations:
  - Spring onion pancakes (春饼)
  - Green rice balls (青团)
  - Seasonal ingredients and herbs
- Background: Gentle gradient washes through seasons
- Decorative: Small nature elements (buds, leaves, flowers)
- Flow: Clockwise movement through seasons

## Color Scheme
- Background: Warm Off-White (#FAF8F0)
- Text: Warm Charcoal (#3D3D3D)
- Seasonal Accents: Sage Green (#87A96B) for spring, Soft Coral (#F4A261) for summer

## Notes
- Show connection between TCM wisdom and modern life
- Use circular, organic composition
- Highlight seasonal food items
- Keep text minimal
- Emphasize tradition meeting technology
