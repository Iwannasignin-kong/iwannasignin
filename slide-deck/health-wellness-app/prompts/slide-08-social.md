# Slide 8: Social Sharing - 社交分享功能

## Style Reference
Watercolor style - soft, hand-painted aesthetic with warm off-white background (#FAF8F0), visible brush strokes, and organic shapes.

## Content
Title: 健康管理，全家参与
Core Message: 健康管理不再是个人事
Features:
- 一键分享食谱
- 传递关爱
- 跨距离健康支持
- 家庭健康共守护

## Visual Composition
- Top: Hand-painted title
- Center: Phone with share interface and multiple family members
  - Three generations illustrated (grandparent, parent, child)
  - Each receiving recipe on their device
- Surrounding: Connection lines painted with brush strokes
- Decorative elements:
  - Small hearts painted with watercolor bleeds
  - Family symbols (home, hands)
  - Gentle steam bowls
- Background: Warm washes of soft coral and dusty rose
- Flow: Radiating from center to family members

## Color Scheme
- Background: Warm Off-White (#FAF8F0)
- Text: Warm Charcoal (#3D3D3D)
- Accents: Soft Coral (#F4A261), Dusty Rose (#E8A0A0), Sage Green (#87A96B)

## Notes
- Focus on family connection and care
- Show multiple generations
- Use warm, loving visual language
- Keep text minimal
- Emphasize sharing and togetherness
