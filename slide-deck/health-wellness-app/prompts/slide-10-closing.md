# Slide 10: Closing - 结语

## Style Reference
Watercolor style - soft, hand-painted aesthetic with warm off-white background (#FAF8F0), visible brush strokes, and organic shapes.

## Content
Main Message: 科技赋能传统养生
Subtitle: 让健康触手可及
Vision: 为每个家庭提供贴心、专业的健康指导
Call to Action: 开启养生食疗新生活

- Center: Large, inspiring main text
- Background: Beautiful watercolor painting combining all elements

- Top: Gentle light wash radiating from center
- Bottom: Small decorative elements (teacup, herbs, phone)
- Overall: Uplifting, hopeful, warm atmosphere
- Flow: Upward movement suggesting growth and wellness

- Accents: All colors blended softly

## Notes
- Create uplifting, inspiring atmosphere
